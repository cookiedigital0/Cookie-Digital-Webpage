FAQ

Quick answers.

How do I pay?

I ask for 50% upfront and the other 50% when we're done. PayPal, Venmo, Zelle, or bank transfer all work.

Do you work outside the tri-state area?

Primarily NY, NJ, and CT — but I'm open to working with anyone. Just reach out and we'll make it work.

What if I need changes after launch?

Starter gets 1 week of fixes. Premium gets 30 days. After that, my maintenance add-on keeps things covered.

Do you do hosting?

I don't sell hosting, but I'll help you pick the right host and get everything set up.

Can I update the site myself?

Yes — especially on the Premium plan with CMS setup. Update content without touching any code.

Not sure which plan?

Just reach out and describe what you're after. I'll tell you honestly which one makes sense.

Ready to get started?

Pick a plan, or just message me and we'll figure it out together.
